Walking Tour of the Historic Centre of Athens

Perfect for first-time visitors, this tour offers the chance to explore Athens' most iconic landmarks without entering the sites themselves. Discover a city with over 3,000 years of history, which only became the capital of Greece in 1832 when the modern state was born.

Itinerary

  • The Trilogy of Athens, Parliament Square, Zappeion Building, Panathenaic Stadium, The Historic Neighborhood of Plaka & Ancient Agora

Tour Duration: 3 hours
