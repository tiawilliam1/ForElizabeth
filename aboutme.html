My background & Education

An officially licensed tourist guide since 2016, I conduct tours throughout Greece and in Athens, the city where I was born and raised. After completing my studies in History and Archaeology at the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens, I continued my education in France at Bordeaux Montaigne University (Management of Cultural Heritage), and at the Sorbonne-INHA Art Institute (Modern Art). Afterwards, I lived in France for eight years, gaining experience in art galleries, auction houses, and museums.

My Vision...

For me, discovering a place is a shared experience: you see the city through my eyes, and I see it through yours. Wandering is an art, and art is all around us in many forms. I enjoy connecting different periods and artists—whether it’s a Cycladic statue that reminds us of a Giacometti sculpture, or a Minoan bull from a Cretan fresco that evokes a Picasso drawing. Curiosity and imagination have no limits, and this is what I aim to inspire in my visitors.

Whether you're interested in ancient ruins, modern art, or local cuisine, I can tailor a tour to suit your interests. Let's explore Greece together and create unforgettable memories!
